United Planters' Association of Southern India · 2015–2024 monthly record

Wayanad carries the only measured production and weather series in the source workbook (2015–2024, monthly). Figures shown for Idukki, Palakkad, Kollam, Thiruvananthapuram, Thrissur and Malappuram are illustrative demo estimates — scaled from Wayanad's real data using Tea Board of India district acreage shares and broad agro-climatic offsets. They are not measured values and must not inform operational or policy decisions. The remaining seven Kerala districts grow no tea and are shown with no data.
